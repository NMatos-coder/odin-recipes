Description

This guacamole recipe gets a tasty kick from cayenne and cilantro.

You can serve it smooth or chunky depending on your tastes.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Herbs
  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl.
  2. Mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic.
  3. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  4. Serve!
