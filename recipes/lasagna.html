Description

This lasagna recipe gets a tasty kick from cayenne and cilantro.

You can serve it depending on your tastes.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Pasta
  • Salt
  • Herbs
  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl.
  2. Mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic.
  3. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  4. Serve!
